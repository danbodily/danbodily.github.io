ROBOTICS

Moving the world one bit at time

I got my start in robotics working for a startup in San Francisco called Otherlab. Day in, day out I worked on the backend of motion planning software that would later be deployed to control an inflatable robotic arm on Mars rover at NASA's Ames Research Center. Along the way I was coached by some awesome engineers, and published a couple conference papers in ICRA (2017). Now I'm a full-time R&D robotics engineer at 3M, doing what I love: translating the nitty gritty math into code and getting stuff to "just work."
